# Working Capital Guardrails

Working capital discipline improves when finance, inventory, and sales teams operate from the same facts. Overstock, discount leakage, and slow collections often show up together rather than separately.

Guardrails for leadership review:
- inventory days should stay aligned to realistic demand
- discounting should not substitute for weak sales qualification
- growth campaigns should be linked to margin-aware outcomes
- supplier commitments should follow cash confidence, not optimism

These guardrails help SMEs grow without stretching the balance of the business too far.
