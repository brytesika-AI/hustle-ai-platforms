# Customer Retention Playbook

Customer retention quality should be reviewed through a commercial lens. The objective is not only service speed but whether service and follow-up are protecting repeat revenue and reducing avoidable churn.

Leadership teams should monitor:
- response speed for priority customers
- escalation volume and root causes
- win-back recovery rate
- conversion from WhatsApp conversations into active deals

The strongest operating rhythm combines customer feedback, sales follow-up quality, and visible accountability across teams.
