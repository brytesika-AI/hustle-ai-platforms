# Cashflow Playbook

Cash discipline for African SMEs is often shaped by delayed customer collections, uneven supplier terms, and the need to fund growth without losing operating resilience.

Management should watch:
- cash in versus cash out trend
- burn concentration by category
- forecast headroom over the next ninety days
- receivables pressure on working capital

Recommended response pattern:
1. tighten collection follow-up
2. stage discretionary spending
3. align inventory purchasing with sell-through reality
4. escalate low-confidence growth initiatives for review
