# Hustle Inventory Intelligence

Hustle Inventory Intelligence is an inventory visibility product focused on stock movement, replenishment timing, supply-side awareness, and working-capital discipline.

## Product Scope

- Stock monitoring
- Replenishment guidance
- Overstock and stockout visibility
- Inventory performance reporting

## Product Structure

This product remains self-contained and may reuse shared platform modules while keeping domain-specific logic within the product boundary.
