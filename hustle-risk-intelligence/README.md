# Hustle Risk Intelligence

Hustle Risk Intelligence is a risk monitoring product for operational oversight, compliance visibility, exposure tracking, and early-warning decision support.

## Product Scope

- Risk scoring and monitoring
- Compliance-oriented visibility
- Exposure tracking
- Escalation support for management teams

## Product Structure

This product remains self-contained and may draw on shared modules without losing ownership of its domain workflows and risk logic.
