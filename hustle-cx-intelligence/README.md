# Hustle CX Intelligence

Hustle CX Intelligence is a customer intelligence product focused on service quality, sentiment visibility, escalation monitoring, and customer performance insights for leadership teams.

## Product Scope

- Sentiment and feedback analysis
- Support performance visibility
- Escalation intelligence
- Retention-oriented insight

## Product Structure

This product remains self-contained and may reuse shared modules for UI, utility logic, model routing, styles, and Cloudflare deployment conventions.
